TWRP RECOVERY

TWRP is an open source, community project and is the leading custom recovery for Android phones. A custom recovery is used for installing custom software on your device. This custom software can include smaller modifications like rooting your device or even replacing the firmware of the device with a completely custom "ROM" like OmniROM.
