Revoked Certificate Workarounds

Important:

This tricks may not work for everyone.

if you happen to be using one of the two iOS apps you can use this fix (doesn't work for all) to bypass the direct download/ certs down issue when it occurs: app must not be deleted from your idevice.

  1. double tap the home button or assistive touch.

  2. open the app via the iOS mutlitasking feature. pending you didn’t swipe the app out.
NOTE: this does not work for everyone.

if direct install (1 of the 2 iOS apps) appears to be down. you need to have the app actually installed. below are the steps for a temporary fix:

  1. turn ON airplane mode (off wifi/ cellular data).

  2. clear safari browser history. (THIS WILL ALSO REMOVE ALL LOGGED-IN EMAIL)
    • open phone settings
    • scroll down and select safari
    • scroll down and select clear history and website data

  3. click app, after loading screen.

  4. check certificate are not revoked.

  5. turn OFF Airplane mode.

  6. run app.

You can prevent your 3rd party app certificates from being revoked using DNS Cloak

How to set-up dnscloak:

  1. Download DNS cloak in app store.
  2. Blacklist the domains below & subdomains in DNSCloak.

    3. ocsp.apple.com
    ppq.apple.com
    appldnld.apple.com
    iadc.qwape.com
    iadsk.apple.com
    mesu.apple.com

  3. Make sure the VPN option is always enabled in your settings. To validate a new certificate quickly disable VPN every time otherwise it won't let you sign the certificate, after the verification - re-enable it.

How to install app when it’s revoked:

If you already have DNSCloak make sure it is turned off before starting.

  • go to ispoofer/ ipogo direct install page
  • Go to your phone home screen. When the app says installing turn on airplane mode. Once the app is done installing, turn airplane mode off
  • Download and setup dnscloak. (instructions to set-up above)
  • With dnscloak running, turn on airplane mode
  • Clear your safari cache.
    • open phone settings
    • scroll down and select safari
    • scroll down and select clear history and website data
  • Turn off airplane mode
  • Close and open settings
  • You should see the certificate is “Verified” now.
  • As long as you keep dnscloak connected your apps will not be revoked!
