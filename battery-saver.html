EXCLUDE CERTAIN APPS FROM BATTERY-SAVER MODE

NOTE:

Settings may vary depending on device brand/ android version. This setting is taken from Samsung J5 (2017) running android 7.x

STEPS:

  1. Open Settings> Device Mentainance.


  2. On Device Maintenance tap Battery.



  3. On Battery tap Unmonitored Apps.



  4. On Unmonitored Apps tap "+ Add Apps".


  5. Add your fake gps app , pokemon go app and whatever app that you don't want to close while in background or overlay app.
