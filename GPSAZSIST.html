GPSAZSIST

This app is a custom plug-in for app ninja's gps joystick. It has basic features for quests, raids, shiny hunting, instant teleport, etc to ease you spoofing life.

by: Aarif Z

FEATURES:

  1. instant teleport (black teleport icon)
  2. location time (clock with gps icon)
  3. cooldown calculator (calculator gps icon)
  4. coords holder (clipboard gps icon)
  5. quest helper (scroll icon)
  6. iv range scanner (iv magnifying glass icon)
  7. shiny hunt helper (sparkling magnifying glass icon)
  8. raid maps (raid boss icon)
  9. auto incubator [root] (white egg icon)
  10. teamgo rocket scanner (r icon)
  11. quest scanner (binocular icon)
  12. notification helper (victory raid icon)
  13. much more! (long press settings)

NOTES:

  1. Works both for rooted and Non rooted but features decrease...for Non rooted.
  2. Non GPS joystick users benefits ...copy coords use trackers etc everything except Teleport..it just copies the coord instead of it for fgl and other.
  3. GPS joystick users benefits Teleport from within app no need to copy paste into joystick.
  4. Rooted GPS joystick users Instant teleport without jumpings + Auto incubator.

HOW TO INSTALL:

  1. dowload the APK below.
  2. disable playstore play protect if ur getting not installed error.
  3. install as a normal APK.
