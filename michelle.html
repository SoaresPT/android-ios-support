The following tricks may stop google play services auto update:

  1. Disable mobile background data for Google play services.

  2. Downgrade google play services.

  3. Disable Google play services storage permission.

  4. Disable google play store.

  5. Clear cache of google play services.

  6. Restart your phone.

NOTE:

If above doesn't work, go to SETTINGS > APPS > 3Dots > (upper rigth) select show SYSTEM APPS. then look for DOWNLOAD MANAGER and disable it.
